Known Limitations

False Positives

VR headsets (Oculus, Meta Quest) share company IDs with Ray-Ban glasses and will trigger false alerts. Proceed with extreme caution.

False Negatives

BLE signals are blocked by walls, bodies, and physical obstacles. Glasses may be present without detection.

Distance Is Rough

Range estimation uses RSSI signal strength only — not precise triangulation. Distance figures are approximations at best.

Randomised MACs

Device MAC addresses rotate periodically. Detection works via BLE company ID only — not hardware address tracking.

Android Only

Requires Android 8.0 (API level 26) or higher. iOS is not supported. No iOS port is planned.

⚠ Do Not Harass

A detected device might be regular glasses. Harassing someone is a criminal offence — potentially more serious than using a covert device. Think before you act.
